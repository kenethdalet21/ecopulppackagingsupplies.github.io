About Ecopulp Packaging Supplies

Your trusted partner in eco-friendly egg packaging solutions since 2010

Our Story

Ecopulp Packaging Supplies began as a small family operation in 2012, driven by a vision to provide high-quality, sustainable egg packaging solutions to local poultry farmers. What started in a small workshop in Carig Norte has grown into one of the leading egg tray manufacturers in the Philippines Nationwide.

Our founder, Joan Lim, recognized the need for reliable, eco-friendly packaging that could protect eggs during transport while being environmentally responsible. With dedication and innovation, we've expanded our production capacity to serve over 200 clients nationwide.

Today, we produce over 500,000+ trays monthly using state-of-the-art machinery and 100% recycled materials. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.

Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Ecopulp Packaging Supplies

Joan Lim

Founder & CEO

With over 13 years of experience in the packaging industry, Joan Lim has dedicated his career to creating sustainable solutions for the agricultural sector. His passion for environmental conservation and commitment to supporting local farmers drove her to establish Ecopulp Packaging Supplies.

Under his leadership, the company has grown from a small operation to a major player in the Philippine egg tray industry.

"Our mission is simple: to provide the best quality egg trays while protecting our environment. Every tray we produce is a step towards a more sustainable future."
