Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order is 100 trays for standard products. For custom orders, minimums may vary. Contact us for specific requirements.

How long does delivery take?

Metro Manila orders are delivered within 1-2 business days. Provincial deliveries take 3-7 days depending on location.

Do you offer custom branding?

Yes! We offer custom printed trays with your logo and branding. Contact us for design options and pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, GCash, PayPal, and cash on delivery (Tuguegarao City & Iguig Area only).
