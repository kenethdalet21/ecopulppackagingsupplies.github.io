Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Home / FAQs

Products

What types of egg trays do you offer?

We offer a wide range of egg trays including: 30-cell and 36-cell standard trays for commercial use, 6, 12, and 18-egg retail cartons with lids, quail egg trays (20-cell and 30-cell), stackable transport trays, and custom solutions with your branding. All our products are made from eco-friendly recycled materials.

Are your egg trays eco-friendly?

Yes! All our egg trays are made from 100% recycled paper pulp and are fully biodegradable. They can be composted or recycled again after use. We're committed to sustainable manufacturing practices and have been certified eco-friendly by environmental authorities.

Can you produce custom-sized trays?

Absolutely! We can manufacture trays with custom cell counts, dimensions, and configurations to meet your specific needs. Custom orders typically require a minimum quantity and may need additional lead time for mold creation. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.

Ordering

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity is 100 trays for standard products. For custom or branded trays, minimums typically start at 500-1,000 trays depending on the specifications. We offer bulk discounts for larger orders - contact us for pricing on orders of 10,000+ trays.

How do I place an order?

You can place orders through our website using the online order form, by calling us at +63 915 674 7277, or by emailing limjoanbcbp19@gmail.com. For regular customers, we can set up standing orders with automatic deliveries on your preferred schedule.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 24 hours of placement at no charge. After production has started, modifications may not be possible for custom orders. Standard product orders can sometimes be modified - please contact us immediately if you need changes.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods: Bank Transfer (BDO, BPI, Metrobank), GCash and Maya mobile payments, PayPal for online transactions, Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard), and Cash on Delivery (Metro Manila only, with 5% COD fee). For business accounts, we offer 30-day credit terms after credit approval.

Do you offer credit terms for businesses?

Yes, we offer 30-day credit terms for qualified business customers. To apply, you'll need to complete a credit application form and provide business registration documents. Credit limits are determined based on order history and business verification.

Are there any bulk order discounts?

Yes! We offer tiered pricing for bulk orders: 5% discount for orders of 1,000-4,999 trays, 10% discount for 5,000-9,999 trays, 15% discount for 10,000+ trays, and custom pricing for orders over 50,000 trays. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote.

Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location: Metro Manila: 1-2 business days (same-day available), Nearby provinces (Cavite, Laguna, Rizal, Bulacan): 2-3 days, Rest of Luzon: 3-5 days, Visayas: 5-7 days, and Mindanao: 5-7 days. Production time for custom orders may add 3-5 additional days.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Free shipping thresholds vary by location: Metro Manila - orders over ₱5,000, Nearby provinces - orders over ₱8,000, Rest of Luzon - orders over ₱15,000, Visayas - orders over ₱20,000, and Mindanao - orders over ₱25,000. Regular customers may qualify for preferential shipping rates.

Can I track my order?

Yes, all orders come with tracking. You'll receive SMS and email updates at each stage: order confirmation, processing, shipped, and delivered. You can also track your order on our website by entering your order number on the Logistics page.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We accept returns for damaged or defective products within 7 days of delivery. Products must be unused and in original packaging. Custom/branded products cannot be returned unless defective. Please document any damage with photos and contact us immediately upon receipt.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return. For damaged items, please include photos. Once approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. We may offer replacement as an alternative.
