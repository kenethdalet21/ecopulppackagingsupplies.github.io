Delivery FAQs

How can I track my order?

You can track your order using the tracking form above or by logging into your account. You'll also receive SMS and email updates at each delivery stage.

What if my order arrives damaged?

Please document the damage with photos and contact us within 24 hours. We offer free replacement for damaged items. Our egg trays are packed with extra protection to minimize damage risk.

Can I request a specific delivery date?

Yes! You can specify a preferred delivery date when placing your order. For bulk orders, we coordinate delivery schedules to match your production needs.

Do you offer pickup options?

Yes, you can pick up your order from our warehouse in Quezon City. Pickup is free and available Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
