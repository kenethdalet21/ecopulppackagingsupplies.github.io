Returns & Refunds

Our commitment to your satisfaction

Home / Returns & Refunds

1. Return Policy Overview

At Ecopulp Packaging Supplies, customer satisfaction is our priority. We stand behind the quality of our products and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

Quality Guarantee: All our products undergo rigorous quality control. If you receive defective products, we'll make it right!

2. Eligible Returns

Products may be returned under the following conditions:

  • Defective Products: Manufacturing defects or damage
  • Wrong Items: Items different from what was ordered
  • Damaged in Transit: Products damaged during shipping
  • Short Shipment: Incorrect quantity received

Return Window

  • Standard Products: 7 days from delivery date
  • Bulk Orders: 14 days from delivery date
  • Custom Orders: See Section 5 for special conditions

3. Non-Returnable Items

Important: The following items cannot be returned:

  • Products opened or used beyond inspection
  • Items with customer-caused damage
  • Products returned after the return window
  • Custom-printed or branded products (unless defective)
  • Items without original packaging
  • Products purchased during clearance sales (marked as final sale)

4. Return Process

Follow these simple steps to return a product:

1

Contact Us

Email returns@ecopulp.com with your order number and reason

2

Get Approval

Receive Return Authorization (RA) number within 24-48 hours

3

Ship Items

Pack securely and ship to our returns center

4

Receive Refund

Refund processed within 5-7 business days after inspection

Required Information for Returns

  • Order number and date of purchase
  • Product name and quantity to return
  • Reason for return with photos (for defects/damage)
  • Preferred resolution (refund, replacement, or store credit)

5. Custom Orders

Custom-printed or specially manufactured products have different return conditions:

  • Returns accepted only for manufacturing defects
  • Design/printing errors on customer-approved proofs are non-returnable
  • Report issues within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photographic evidence of defects

6. Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed using the original payment method:

  • Bank Transfer: 5-7 business days
  • GCash: 3-5 business days
  • Credit Card: 7-14 business days (depending on bank)
  • Store Credit: Immediate (can be used on next purchase)

Refund Amounts

  • Defective/Wrong Items: Full refund including shipping
  • Customer Change of Mind: Not applicable (B2B policy)
  • Partial Returns: Pro-rated refund based on items returned

7. Replacement Policy

For eligible returns, you may choose replacement instead of refund:

  • Same product replacement at no additional cost
  • Exchange for different product (price difference applies)
  • Priority processing for replacement orders
  • Free shipping on replacement items

8. Return Shipping

Who Pays for Return Shipping?

  • Defective/Wrong Items: Ecopulp Packaging Supplies covers return shipping
  • Damaged in Transit: Ecopulp Packaging Supplies covers return shipping
  • Other Reasons: Customer pays return shipping

Return Address:

9. Inspection Process

Upon receiving your return:

  1. Items inspected within 2 business days
  2. Verification of defect/damage claim
  3. Approval or denial notification sent via email
  4. Refund/replacement processed upon approval

10. Cancellations

Order cancellation policy:

  • Before Processing: Full refund, no fee
  • After Processing (not shipped): Full refund, 5% admin fee
  • After Shipping: Must follow return process
  • Custom Orders in Production: 50% refund maximum

11. Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with a return decision:

  1. Contact our Customer Service Manager at manager@ecopulp.com
  2. Provide order details and previous communication
  3. Resolution provided within 3 business days
  4. Escalation to owner (Joan Lim) available upon request
