Shipping Policy

Learn about our delivery process and shipping rates

Home / Shipping Policy

1. Shipping Overview

Ecopulp Packaging Supplies is committed to delivering your orders safely and on time. We ship throughout the Philippines and offer various shipping options to meet your business needs.

Free Shipping: Enjoy FREE delivery on all orders over ₱10,000 within Metro Manila!

2. Processing Time

Orders are processed within the following timeframes:

  • Standard Orders: 1-2 business days
  • Custom/Bulk Orders: 3-5 business days (depending on specifications)
  • Express Processing: Same day (order before 12:00 PM, additional fee applies)

Processing time does not include delivery time. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • Email confirmation with tracking number
  • SMS notification (if phone number provided)
  • Access to track your order on our Logistics page

You can also contact our customer service team for order updates.

6. Delivery Partners

We work with trusted logistics partners to ensure safe delivery:

  • Ecopulp Direct Fleet: For Metro Manila bulk deliveries
  • 2GO Express: Nationwide coverage
  • J&T Express: Provincial deliveries
  • LBC: Island destinations
  • JRS Express: Remote areas

7. Delivery Requirements

For successful delivery:

  • Ensure complete and accurate delivery address
  • Provide a working contact number
  • Someone must be available to receive the package
  • For businesses, specify receiving hours

8. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery cannot be completed:

  • First attempt: Re-delivery scheduled for next business day
  • Second attempt: Package held at nearest hub (5 business days)
  • After 5 days: Package returned to sender; restocking and reshipping fees may apply

9. Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit:

  • Document damage with photos before opening
  • Report within 24 hours of receiving
  • Contact us at logistics@ecopulp.com
  • We will arrange replacement or refund

10. Special Shipping Requests

For special requirements, please contact our logistics team:

  • Scheduled delivery (specific date/time)
  • Multiple delivery locations
  • International shipping inquiries
  • Large volume/freight shipments
